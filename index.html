During our commute to school or work, there is always a homeless man sleeping on the train seats or on the sidewalk. It is a sad sight to see. People who have lost everything and now resort to living on the streets. The government does little to help these people. We are here to fix this problem and turn the homeless people into Home-More people.

How many homeless people are?

Why we should help

Even though these people are homeless now, they were not always homeless. They might've been homeless due to death of their caretakers, laid off of their job, or they were orphaned. What we are trying to say is that most homeless people experienced a series of unfortunate events which led to where they are now. They haven't gotten the opportunity to turn their lives around, but we are here to give them that opportunity many seek. By giving them housing, they don't have to worry about freezing during the winter or the overwhelming heat of the summer. Giving them housing could allow them to seek out jobs and finally turn their lives around.
