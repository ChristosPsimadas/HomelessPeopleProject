Our Goal

Our goal is to remove all homeless people from the streets of New York City and relocate them to a better environment. Many New York City apartments are left vacant and empty. The only purpose of these apartments is for the rich to avoid taxes and collect dust. We purpose to buy or legally obtain these apartments inorder to redistrubite them to the homeless. This would help the homeless have shelter and allow them to seek jobs or other forms of employment to get them back on their feet. If this strategy doesn't work, we could create tiny houses or small homes in areas where there is empty land.
